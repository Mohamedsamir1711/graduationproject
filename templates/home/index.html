Our services

Cardiology

Diagnosis and treatment of heart disease

Real-time Heart Rate Monitoring

Patients can continuously track their heart rate in real-time, allowing them to stay informed about their heart health.

Automated Alerts & Notifications

Instant alerts can be sent to both patients and doctors if the heart rate exceeds or drops below normal levels, helping in early detection of potential issues.

Historical Data & Trends Analysis

Patients can view historical heart rate data and trends over days, weeks, or months to monitor long-term heart health.

AI-Based Health Insights & Recommendations

Using AI, the system can analyze heart rate patterns and provide personalized recommendations for improving heart health.

Doctor's Dashboard & Remote Consultation

Doctors can access patient data remotely, allowing them to monitor multiple patients and provide virtual consultations based on real-time and historical heart rate data.

Emergency Response Integration

In case of abnormal heart rate patterns, the system can automatically notify emergency contacts or healthcare providers to take immediate action.

Medication & Lifestyle Reminders

Patients can receive reminders for medications, exercise, and lifestyle changes based on their heart rate trends.

Technical Support

A dedicated support team is available around the clock to assist patients and healthcare providers with any technical issues, ensuring seamless operation of the heart rate monitoring system.
